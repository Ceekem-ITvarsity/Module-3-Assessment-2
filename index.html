Our Story

Founded in 2020, Rustic Table was born from a passion for authentic, honest food that brings people together. Our chefs combine time-honored techniques with innovative approaches to create memorable dining experiences that celebrate the bounty of local ingredients.

We believe that great food should be accessible to all, which is why we work directly with local farmers and producers to bring you the freshest ingredients at reasonable prices.
